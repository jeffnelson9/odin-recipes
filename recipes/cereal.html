Cereal, Seriously

Borne of equal parts ambition and creativity, cereal satisfies the soul

Once considered the hallmark of a balanced breakfast, when paired with various nutritious foods, cereal re-emerges as a late night contender for the perfect snack.

Crunchy oats and creamy milk converge to battle post-coital cravings like only vice and gluttony can. Grab a bowl and your favorite spoon, it's time to walk the razor's edge between satisfying and soggy.

You will need

  • your favorite bowl
  • your favorite spoon
  • your favorite cereal
  • milk or milk alternative of your choice
  • fruit, nuts or granola (optional)

Directions

  1. Pour the cereal into the bowl
  2. Add optional toppings
  3. Add milk to desired level
  4. Crunch loudly, unashamed, until your lover reconsiders their choices
