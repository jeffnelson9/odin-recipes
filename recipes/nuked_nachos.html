Nuked Nachos

A truly insta-worthy microwave meal, ready to serve in just 30 seconds

A definitive original, the foundation other nacho recipes are built upon. A return to its roots, this dish exemplifies the awesome power of microwave radiation. The warmth and simplicity of this dish cannot be overstated.

Best enjoyed with friends, this dish is as scalable as your plateware allows. As you progress through each iteration, take a moment to modify the recipe based on individual preferences. Variations are limitless with such a blank canvas. Let your imagination soar. This crispy creation is best served with jarred salsa, as desired.

Stand at least three feet from microwave during cooking to limit exposure to harmful radiation. Unnaturally short cooking times are not without risk.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Cheese (fresh or otherwise)
  • Chopped veggies or meats (optional)

Cooking Directions

  1. Plate tortilla chips in desired quantity
  2. Shred cheese if necessary
  3. Top tortilla chips with optional veggies or meat and shredded cheese--enough to cover chips
  4. Place dish in microwave and cook on high setting for approximately 30 seconds. Repeat as necessary until cheese is melted
  5. Enjoy with jarred salsa or a dip of your choice

Bon apetit!
