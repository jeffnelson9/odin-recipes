Basic Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's Pancake Mix
  • 2 Tbsp. cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup water, plus 2 Tbsp.
  • One large egg, free range AF

You Will Also Need

  • Pure maple syrup (NH, ME, VT)
  • Butter or butter substitute
  • The latest smartphone with Instagram installed

Directions:

  1. Read over the box instructions
  2. Whisk egg in mixing bowl
  3. Add water and oil to mixing bowl. Stir to combine
  4. Add pancake mix to bowl according to box instructions
  5. Retrieve box from trash container and reread instructions
  6. Preheat a medium-sized frying pan over medium heat while batter rests
  7. Add 1/4-cup of batter to preheated pan. Flip when bubbles form on edges
  8. Stack 3-4 finished pancakes on plate and top with syrup and butter
  9. Experiment with various instagram filters on smartphone until pancakes appear "so good"
  10. Post photo with a concise, clever caption to remind the world that you not only work hard, but play hard as well
